Game Modes – Best Picks Explained

Gem Grab

Key Traits: Mid control, sustain, comeback potential.

Best Picks: Spike (S), Emz (S), Gene (A)

Why: Spike and Emz lock down choke points, while Gene provides safe pulls and healing utility.

Brawl Ball

Key Traits: Wall pressure, team wipes.

Best Picks: Shelly (S), Mortis (S), Frank (S), Stu (A)

Why: Shelly and Frank dominate close-range fights, Mortis enables clutch goals, Stu adds mobility.

Knockout

Key Traits: No respawns, mistake punishment.

Best Picks: Belle (A), Mina (S), Piper (A)

Why: Long-range pressure and safe poke win rounds without risk.

Heist

Key Traits: Raw DPS and safe damage.

Best Picks: Colt (S), Spike (S), Jessie (A)

Why: Colt shreds safes, Spike controls defense, Jessie snowballs with turret pressure.

Hot Zone

Competitive Meta Summary (Final Grade)

This meta is defined by Buffies-driven consistency and overtuned kits. S-tier brawlers either remove counterplay (Buffies) or overwhelm it (Mina, Meeple). Drafting revolves around denying Buffies value and preventing snowball.

Counterplay & Draft Notes

Against Colt

Use long-range poke (Piper, Belle) and avoid clumped walls. Deny wall break value early.

Against Spike

High-mobility brawlers (Stu, Max) reduce area denial effectiveness.

Against Mortis

Tanky A-tier picks with CC force Mortis to disengage.

Against Shelly / Frank

Open maps and sustained range pressure limit close-range dominance.

Against Mina / Meeple

Ban priority in competitive drafts; counterplay is unreliable.

A Tier Role Clarification

A-tier brawlers are mode specialists. They thrive when conditions are correct but lack universal dominance.

  • Gene: Safe mid control, falls off versus Buffies pressure
  • Belle: Excellent poke, limited carry potential
  • Stu: Mobility-based playmaking, countered by control
  • Piper: High skill sniper, map-dependent
  • Sandy: Zone utility, weak into burst

Competitive Draft Priority

  1. Ban Meeple or Mina
  2. Secure Buffies brawler
  3. Draft control A-tier for synergy
  4. Draft counters last
